Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to create an intuitive guide for people to come and learn to make mechanical keyboards without being confused by lingo or words that the mechanical keyboard community tends to use. It will also explain the purpose for each step in order to make it easy to understand what they need to do to make it the way that they want.

Audience

The intended audience for this website is DIY enthusiasts and hobbyists interested in creating mechanical keyboards, especially those who are new and need straightforward guidance to get started.
